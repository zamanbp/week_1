KGPTC Westhill

About KGPTC

The Kerala Govt Polytechnic College, Westhill was established in 1946 , one of the oldest polytechnic colleges in Kerala. The college was inaugurated by the honourable Sri.V V Giri , BAR-AT-LAW , minister for industries and labour , Govt. of Madras on 23rd November 1946. Sri.Sreenivas Rao was the first principal. Over the years the intake of students increased and as of now with six full-fledged departments offering six engineering diploma programmes such as Civil Engineering, Computer Engineering, Chemical Engineering, Mechanical Engineering, Electrical & Electronics Engineering, Tool & Die Engineering under the Department of Technical Education, Kerala.

The college has around 1200 students , 96 teaching staffs and 28 non teaching staffs. All the department of college are adequately staffed and well equipped to meet the needs of latest technological trends and international standards.

Departments

  1. Computer Engineering
  2. Computer Engineering (HI)
  3. Chemical Engineering
  4. Mechanical Engineering
  5. Civil Engineering
  6. Tool & Die Engineering
  7. Electrical and Electronics Engineering

Vision and Mission


Vision

  • To create a renowned placement and training division that prepares students for the global industry by providing comprehensive training, counselling, and placement services.

Mission

  • To enhance the employability skills of students through quality training, counseling, and guidance.
  • To establish and maintain strong industry-academia relationships for providing students with industrial exposure, internships, and final placements.
  • To promote entrepreneurship among students by providing them with relevant skills, knowledge, and resources.
  • To provide students with a platform to showcase their talents, creativity, and innovations through various competitions, projects, and events.
  • To create a nurturing and inclusive environment that supports the holistic development of students.
