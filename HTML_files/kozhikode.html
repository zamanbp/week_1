Other Attractions

Kozhikode, also known as Calicut, has a rich history that dates back to ancient times. It was a major trading hub for spices and textiles, attracting merchants from around the world. The city's unique blend of cultures is reflected in its architecture, festivals, and local cuisine.

Don't miss the opportunity to savor Kozhikode's famous Malabar biryani, banana chips, and various seafood delicacies.
